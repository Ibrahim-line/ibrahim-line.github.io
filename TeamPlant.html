Fiona the Fiddle Leaf Fig

Fiona loves bright, indirect light and thrives in a warm, humid environment. Her large leaves add elegance to any room.

Sammy the Snake Plant

Sammy is tough and low-maintenance, perfect for beginners. He helps purify the air and can tolerate low light.

Lily the Peace Lily

Lily is graceful and brings peace to any space. She loves shady corners and rewards you with beautiful white blooms.

Spencer the Spider Plant

Spencer is energetic and adaptable, producing baby plantlets that can grow into new plants easily. A true team player!
